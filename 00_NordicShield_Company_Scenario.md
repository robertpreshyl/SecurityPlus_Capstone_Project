# NordicShield Technologies Oy
## *A Comprehensive Security+ (SY0-701) Capstone Project*

---

## Project Overview

This capstone project demonstrates comprehensive knowledge of all five CompTIA Security+ SY0-701 domains through a realistic enterprise security scenario. You will act as a Junior Security Analyst helping a Finnish SME establish security posture during global expansion.

---

## Company Overview

**NordicShield Technologies Oy** is a Finnish small-medium enterprise (SME) founded in 2018, specializing in **sustainable data center cooling solutions** and **IoT-enabled building management systems**. Headquartered in **Turku, Finland**, the company has grown from 35 to 120 employees and is valued at €25 million.

### Core Business

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Industry** | Green Technology / Data Center Infrastructure |
| **Founded** | 2018, Turku, Finland |
| **Specialty** | Sustainable cooling solutions & IoT building management |
| **Employees** | 120 (growing to 150+ with expansion) |
| **Valuation** | €25 million |
| **Clients** | 45+ data centers across Nordic region |
| **IoT Devices** | 500+ sensors deployed at client sites |

## The Situation: Global Expansion

In January 2026, NordicShield secured Series B funding of €15 million to expand globally. The expansion plan includes:

| Region | Location | Office Type | Timeline | Employees | Primary Function |
|--------|----------|-------------|----------|-----------|------------------|
| **EU (HQ)** | Turku, Finland | Headquarters | Existing | 85 | All operations |
| **EU Expansion** | Amsterdam, Netherlands | Regional Office | Q2 2026 | 15 | EU Sales & Support |
| **Americas** | Austin, Texas, USA | Sales & Support Hub | Q3 2026 | 12 | Americas Market |
| **Africa** | Kigali, Rwanda | Tech Support & Dev | Q4 2026 | 8 | Development & Support |

---

## Your Role

You are **Junior Security Analyst** hired to join the newly formed **Information Security Team**. The CISO, **Elina Virtanen**, has tasked you with helping establish the company's security posture as they expand globally.

> **"We've grown quickly, and security has been reactive. Now, we need to be proactive. I need you to help me assess where we are, identify gaps, and build a security program that scales across continents and complies with multiple regulatory frameworks."**
> — Elina Virtanen, CISO

## Regulatory Compliance Requirements
