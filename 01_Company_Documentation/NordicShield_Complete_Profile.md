# PART 1: COMPANY BACKSTORY

## 1.1 Founding Story

**NordicShield Technologies Oy** was founded in March 2018 by three Finnish engineers who met while working at Nokia's former facilities in Oulu:

- **Mikko Lahtinen** (CEO) - Former Nokia thermal engineer, 15 years experience
- **Dr. Anja Korhonen** (CTO) - PhD in sustainable energy systems from Aalto University
- **Tomas Heikkinen** (COO) - Operations background from Wärtsilä

The founders identified a critical gap in the market: **data centers consume 1-2% of global electricity**, with 40% of that used just for cooling. They developed a revolutionary liquid cooling system using Finland's cold climate and innovative heat exchange technology that reduces cooling energy consumption by 60%.

### The Breakthrough (2019)

In late 2019, NordicShield secured a contract with **Telia Finland** to pilot their cooling solution at a Helsinki data center. The results exceeded expectations:
- 62% reduction in cooling costs
- PUE (Power Usage Effectiveness) improved from 1.8 to 1.15
- Zero downtime during implementation

This success attracted attention from Google, who visited in early 2020.

### COVID Pivot & IoT Expansion (2020)

During COVID-19, data center demand skyrocketed. NordicShield couldn't install on-site, so they developed **NordicSense** - an IoT platform that:
- Monitors temperature, humidity, and airflow remotely
- Predicts cooling failures before they occur
- Optimizes energy consumption using machine learning

This pivot transformed NordicShield from a hardware company to a **hardware + software + IoT** company.
